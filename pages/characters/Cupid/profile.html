Backstory

As one of the reindeer on Santa's coveted sleigh team, you enjoy your status at the North Pole immensely. It gives you great joy to be part of the sleigh team that annually delivers toys and gifts to the children of the world. Additionally, you get great satisfaction from the comradeship you have with your teammates and you feel as though you have found the love of your life on the sleigh team, Comet.

While the members of the sleigh team have remained constant for many years, this year may be different. With the growing global population and increasing demand for toys, Santa is very worried that the current toy production and sleigh speed will not be adequate to meet demand. Because of this, Santa has stated that she will be making some changes to increase efficiency on all fronts. This has many elves and reindeer, including you, worried for their positions.

To help ease the worry, you have secretly been participating in speed drills with Dusty who hopes to one day join the sleigh team. You feel these drills will give you an added advantage over your teammates if one of you is to be replaced because of your speed. Recently, Rudolph has been very flirtatious with you. You have shunned Rudolph's advances and let him and others know that you are in love with Comet.

When Comet found out about your love for her, she professed the same feelings of affection for you. However, your relationship has not progressed, partly because of the recent elf incident. Just last week, Hermey was trampled by a reindeer, leaving him in a coma and Hermey's wife, Ivy, full of grief. Santa has vowed to take flying rights away from the guilty reindeer. Currently, Comet is the prime suspect because of her amazing speed, but she claims to be innocent. Ever since the accident, Comet has been doing everything to clear her name, but it does not seem to be working.

A heart-strong reindeer, you are not the only one in the North Pole who has commitment and marriage on your mind. Recently, you befriended Connie Clause who suggested to you that she is secretly in love. However, you overheard Mr. Claus talking about her marriage wishes for Connie. While Mr. Claus wants Connie to marry for true love, she also wants Connie to marry someone who can take over Santa's duties when Santa retires. As part of this plan, Santa has recruited Kristy Krangle to come and court Connie. While you applaud their efforts, Kristy and Connie have little in common including romantic chemistry. It is clear to you that Mr. Claus and Santa's first priority is Santa's legacy, not Connie's happiness.

As the Holly, Jolly Christmas Party is quickly approaching, you are happy to attend. With everyone in attendance, you think it will be the perfect place to search out the true culprit for Hermey's incident. Additionally, you hope the festive spirit will help Comet to forget her worries and enjoy some time together!

Acting Your Part

As a heart-strong reindeer, you are looking forward to the party to spend some time with your girlfriend, Comet. You plan to use the party to get Comet to loosen up and commit to you. Additionally, you are very intrusive and curious about the romantic relationships of your peers. You hope the Holly, Jolly Christmas Party will create a laid back atmosphere where you can gossip about relationships.

Costumes Suggestions

For the party, you will dress in your reindeer attire, which may include brown clothing, antlers, collar, jingle bells and, possibly, your most festive Christmas sweater.
